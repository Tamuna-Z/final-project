How does churchkhela affect your health?

Churchkhela is a traditional sausage-shaped candy made by repeatedly dipping a long string of nuts in tatara – a mixture of flour, sugar and Badagi (concentrated fresh grape juice)

It is rich in beneficial fatty acids. Regulates cholesterol level and blood pressure, increases the body's resistance, strengthens the immune system, is effective in anemia and helminth infections.

Hazelnut and nut are considered to have a beneficial effect on the mind and heart. Contains vitamins PP, A, B1, B2, B5, B6, B9, C, E, K, which activates brain function, improves memory and the ability to concentrate.
